About me

I'm currently a 3th year graduate student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. I received my Undergraduate Bachelors of Science at Fairfield University in Computer Science and Mathematics.

My current research is in the field of Computer Graphics working with Barb Cutler on visualizations and tools for architectural daylighting. I have a board range of research interest including computer graphics, simulations, and visualizations. I also did some undergraduate research at the University of Connecticut in Bioinformatics.
